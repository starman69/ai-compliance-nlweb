# Automated Employment Decision Tools (AEDT)

 Local Law 144 of 2021 regarding automated employment decision tools (&ldquo;AEDT&rdquo;) prohibits employers and employment agencies from using an automated employment decision tool unless the tool has been subject to a bias audit within one year of the use of the tool, information about the bias audit is publicly available, and certain notices have been provided to employees or job candidates.
 Read Local Law 144 of 2021 
 Read Rule .
 Note: DCWP will begin enforcement of this law and rule on July 5, 2023.

## File a Complaint

 You can report an employer or employment agency that used an AEDT but did not:

 Make sure the required bias audit was done 
 Post a summary of the results of the bias audit 
 Give required notices 
 
 Submit your complaint online . Note: You do NOT need to create an account to file a complaint with DCWP. However, an account will allow you to more easily track and make updates to your complaint.
