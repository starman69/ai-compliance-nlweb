# Cloud Security Alliance — AI Security Guidance

## AI Safety Initiative
The Cloud Security Alliance (CSA) AI Safety Initiative develops and freely distributes trusted
best practices for deploying AI, with an initial focus on generative AI. It builds on CSA's
work establishing the trusted cloud ecosystem and convenes governments, industry, and NGOs for
rapid collaboration.

### Objectives
- **Best-practice development** — create and freely distribute guidance for responsible AI.
- **Customer confidence** — help organizations of all sizes adopt AI responsibly via risk-
  mitigation frameworks.
- **Industry self-regulation** — complement government AI assurance programs with collaborative
  industry oversight.
- **Ethical leadership** — address societal and ethical implications of advanced AI.

## Research and governance structure
- **AI Governance & Compliance Working Group** — leads technical and policy research.
- **AI Controls Matrix** — a controls framework for securing AI systems (mapped to cloud
  security controls).
- **Discussion communities** — stakeholder forums for knowledge sharing.

### Key research areas
- Security implications of generative AI / large language models.
- AI in regulated contexts (e.g., healthcare) and associated privacy/security standards.
- Responsible AI implementation requiring cross-organizational collaboration.
- Data-center and cloud cybersecurity impacts from LLM usage.

## Participation
Organizations engage by joining the AI governance/compliance working group, participating in
discussion communities, and accessing CSA's published AI security research and guidance.
