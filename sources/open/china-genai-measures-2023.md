# China — Interim Measures for the Management of Generative AI Services (2023)

Interim Measures for the Management of Generative Artificial Intelligence Services, issued by
the Cyberspace Administration of China (CAC) with six other ministries; **effective 15 August
2023**.

## Issuing authorities
CAC; National Development and Reform Commission; Ministries of Education; Science and
Technology; Industry and Information Technology; Public Security; and the State Administration
of Radio and Television.

## Scope
Applies to **generative AI services** that provide text, image, audio, video, or other content
to the public in mainland China. Excludes internal R&D not offered as a public service.

## Key obligations for providers
- **Content** — generated content must adhere to core socialist values and must not incite
  subversion of state power, undermine national unity, promote terrorism/extremism, or contain
  obscene/false/harmful material.
- **Non-discrimination** — in algorithm design, training-data selection, and operation,
  prevent discrimination by ethnicity, belief, nationality, region, sex, age, occupation, or
  health.
- **IP and personal information** — use lawfully sourced data and foundation models; respect
  intellectual property and others' rights; protect users' input and usage records.
- **Accuracy and labeling** — take measures to improve the accuracy and reliability of
  generated content; **label** AI-generated images/video per the deep-synthesis rules.
- **Security assessment and algorithm filing** — services with public-opinion or social-
  mobilization capacity must complete a security assessment and algorithm filing.

## Provider responsibilities
Sign service agreements defining rights/obligations; establish complaint and reporting
mechanisms; cooperate with regulators (disclosing training-data sources and algorithm
mechanisms on request); promptly remove illegal content and optimize models; and prevent minor
users' over-reliance/addiction. Violations may draw warnings, rectification orders, suspension,
or — for serious cases — criminal liability.
